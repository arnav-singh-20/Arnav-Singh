Chapter 4.1 — "...a system that catches fraud"
Credit Card Fraud Detection Model
AUC-PR: 0.9752Recall: 87.5%False Alarm: 0.152%£5,338 saved

I built a pipeline that could catch financial fraud before it happened — with SMOTE to fix the data imbalance, XGBoost to power the predictions, and SHAP to explain every decision the model made.
